Parade Route

Begins at Tulare and P Street.

  • Start out going northwest on P St toward Mariposa Mall. (go 0.18 miles)
  • Turn left onto Fresno St. (go 0.38 miles)
  • Turn left onto M St. (go 0.18 miles)
  • Turn right onto Tulare St. (go 0.19 miles)
  • 1800 TULARE ST is on the left. (go 0 miles)

Ends Chukchansi Park, 1800 Tulare St, Fresno, CA 93721 US

Announcers and Grandstand locations:

Television and Main Grandstand – In front of Fresno City Hall on P St. 2600 Fresno St.

Second Announcing Stand – In front of the Veterans Memorial Museum 2425 Fresno St.
